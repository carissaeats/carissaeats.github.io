Step aside, Colonel Sanders. The new K.F.C. is in town.

Exploding in popularity in the last few years, Korean Fried Chicken has officially arrived in America.

but what...is Korean Fried Chicken?

What makes Korean fried chicken different from American is the double-fry: the first fry is to cook it all the way through, and the second fry, which is at a higher temperature, is to get the extra crunchy crust we all know & love. This crispy, flaky crust is necessary to withstand the thick, sweet, garlicky, and depending on if you like, spicy sauce that coats the outside.

Mama Chicken

Palisades Park: 252 Broad Ave, Palisades Park, NJ 07650

Mama Chicken makes some of the best Korean-inspired dishes in town. They make the best cheat day eats, and it's hard to disagree. With dishes like their Cheesy Kimchi-Bacon Fried Rice to their Rose Chicken (don't skip this!), Mama Chicken is a great introduction to Korean food for newbies. And, they serve their crispy padak (shown above!) with honey mustard and sweet & spicy sauce, topped with a boatload of scallions. Bonus points for the free unlimited tteokboki (spicy Korean rice cakes) that's included.
